  • Voicemail Help

  • Access your voicemail by selecting and holding 1 from the call screen.
    Follow the prompts to create a new 7-15 digit voicemail password.
    If you called the voicemail system, press the # key and hang up.
    Turn your wireless device off and back on.
    Attempt to access your voicemail again.

    If you're unable to access Visual Voicemail, try calling your voicemail box by selecting and holding 1 from the call screen.

    Verify wireless coverage
    To verify that you have enough signal strength to connect to voicemail, view wireless coverage maps:
    Visual Voicemail requires a wireless cellular data connection.
    Some smartphones require a 4G LTE data connection in order to access and manage Visual Voicemail.

  • Data Usage Support

    Get info on high-speed data and your wireless plan.

  • Some of our wireless plans come with a set amount of data on your plan. Plans give you data that can only be used for a specific line or device. Please ensure you have not used more than the allotted highspeed data available with your plan.

  • On an unlimited plan? If a lot of devices in your area are using mobile data at once, it can put a strain on our network. This is called network congestion, data speeds may be a little slower due to all the traffic. 

  • Another common reason for data slower than you'd expect is determined by your location in regards to a tower. Check this tower map to confirm you are within the network map.
