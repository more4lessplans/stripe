"What are speeds/coverage like?"

Our Bottomless Data Plan gives you service in Canada, USA, and Mexico. We do not have any infrustructure of our own. We operate entirely off major carrier networks. The network and speeds are all top tier with full LTE service. Check the appropriate websites for speeds and coverage maps.

"Can I get a new phone?" 

Not with us. Bring Your own device only. This will actually save you money.

"I'm already with a differet provider, am I still eligible?""

Yes. This plan is available to all who bring their own device! Check with your current provider to see if you are in a contract.   

"Can I keep my number?"

Our bottomless data plans come with a USA number, and porting is not available. Although you can still keep your canadian number if you have a dual sim phone or if you use a VOIP service!

"How do I pay every month?"

When you activate you will be walked through how to set up autopay! PayPal, debit/credit and automatic bank withdrawls are all available!     

"Can I use this for my home internet with an LTE router?"

This plan is advertised as a mobile phone plan. We do not offer any guarantee that this will work in a device that is not a mobile phone. Personal hotspot is also not guaranteed at full speed. To find out if your cellular phone is compatible with our network, click here.

"Will my plan expire or price increase?"

Once you sign up your plan is locked in. No price increases will ever happen for an active line. Pricing is always increasing so order now to lock in your price!

"I have a dual sim phone, how does this work."

There is no way to port a number to our Bottomless Data Plan. To keep your preferred number we commonly suggest a secondary line. Newer iPhones and many other Androids have dual-sim capability. This allows 2 lines to be active simultaneously. The most popular combo is our plan + the $9 talk and text plan (Freedom Mobile)
