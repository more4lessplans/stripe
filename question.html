Frequently Asked Questions



Will This Plan Work for Me?

We can guarantee service for all Canadians who sign up and bring a compatible device!

Is It Really Unlimited?

Yes! No data caps or restrictions. Many customers use over 200GB of blazing fast data every month.>compatible device!

What are Speeds/Coverage Like?

The Bottomless Data Plan gives you service in Canada, USA, and Mexico. We do not have any infrustructure of our own, as we are not a cellular carrier. We manage your access to the major carrier networks. The network and speeds are all top tier LTE/5G service from "The Big Three".

Can I Get a New Phone? 

Not with us. Bring Your own device only. This will actually save you money.

I am Already With a Differet Provider. Can I Still Register?

Yes. This plan is available to all who bring their own device! Check with your current provider to see if you are in a contract.   

Can I Keep My Number?

Our bottomless data plans come with a USA number, and porting is not available. Although you can still keep your canadian number if you have a dual sim phone or if you use a VOIP service!

How Do I Make Payments?

When you activate you will be walked through how to set up autopay! We accept all major credit cards.

Will This Work With My Router?

We do not offer any guarantee that this will work in a device that is not a mobile phone. It is possible, but not recommended. To find out if your cellular phone is compatible with the network, click here.

Will My Plan Expire or Increase in Price?

Once you sign up for auto-pay your plan is locked in. No price increases will ever happen for an active line signed up for autopay. Pricing is always increasing so order now to lock in your price!

Can I Change My Plan Mid-Term?

You prepay for 30 days of our services upon activation, any unused services from your current plan is forfeited if you change to another plan. We recommend waiting until your term is complete to change your plan - a new SIM card may be required to change plans.  

I Have a Dual SIM Phone. How Can I Keep My Number?

Dual-SIM devices allow multiple lines to be active simultaneously. There is no way to port a number to our Bottomless Data Plans. To keep your preferred number get a secondary line.
The most popular combo is our plan + the $9 talk and text plan. (Freedom Mobile)

How Long To Get My SIM Card?

We mail out SIM cards the same day or next day, via Canada Post mail. eSIM is delivered digitally within one business day. Contact us for an estimated delivery.

What Happens If I Cancel?

You can cancel your service at anytime via our self serve customer portal, and since it is a prepaid service - your service will automatically stop working at the end of your current usage cycle. There are no fees when cancelling. A new SIM card may be requires if you decide to reinstate your service.

Does Home Internet Need a Scheduled Installation?

No! The Home internet service is plug-n-play and fully portable. You are not restricted to using it at one location. You can even connect a dedicated router to our modem using an Ethernet cable.
